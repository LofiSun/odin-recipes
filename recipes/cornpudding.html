Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 15oz Can Corn Kernels
  • 1 15oz Can Creamed Corn
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 8.5oz Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
  • 1 Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Basil
  • 2 Tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 Tsp Ginger
  • 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tsp Parika
  • 1 tsp Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheight.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix until combined.
  3. Add to a 9x9 Baking Dish.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes. The corn pudding should be puffy, slightly brown and the center firm.
  5. Let cool for at least 10 minutes.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
